# Planning workspace

The Planning Workspace is a tabbed editor panel that provides a full-screen view of your book's structure and story elements.

## Themes tab

Define your book's central question, synopsis, and thematic elements. Each theme can be linked to specific chapters, helping you track which parts of your manuscript explore which ideas. You can also set your book's working title and genres directly from this tab.

## Outline tab

A grid view of your entire outline hierarchy with columns for title, status, and synopsis. Click any node to open it in the editor.

## Cards tab

A corkboard-style view showing each chapter as a synopsis card — useful for visual planning and getting a bird's-eye view of your narrative flow.

## Characters tab

Full character profile management: create, edit, and delete characters. Each profile stores name, aliases, description, and freeform notes as a markdown file with YAML frontmatter. LeanQuill automatically scans your manuscript on save and links characters to chapters where their name or aliases appear.

## Threads tab

Story threads represent narrative arcs, subplots, or recurring motifs. Each thread can be associated with specific chapters, letting you track which threads are active where in your manuscript.

## Places tab

Setting and location profiles linked to scenes. *(This tab is a placeholder — full implementation is coming in a future update.)*
